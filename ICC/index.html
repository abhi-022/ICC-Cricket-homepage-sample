Brendon McCullum announces retirement from cricket

New Zealand superstar Brendon McCullum has announced that he will retire from cricket at the conclusion of the Global T20 Canada. "As much as I am proud of what I've achieved in my 20-year professional career - more than I ever could have dreamed of when I first entered the game - I have felt the drive to keep going harder to maintain in recent months," he said in a statment posted on Twitter.
